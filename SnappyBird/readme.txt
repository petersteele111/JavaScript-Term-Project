Snappy bird is a fast paced game for all ages. Use the up and down arrow keys in order to move the bird in between the pipes as the appear. Be careful though, if you hit the pipes or the ground, you lose!
No current issues or problems with the game. It is fully functional
Future versions may include sounds for hitting objects and tilting up or down as the bird falls or ascends.

Snake is a fast paced game for all ages. Use the arrow keys in order to move the snake around the screen and collect the red dots (Food). Be careful though, if you hit yourself or the walls, you lose!
No current issues or problems with the game. It is fully functional
Future versions may include sounds for collecting food and the ability to save your high score.
